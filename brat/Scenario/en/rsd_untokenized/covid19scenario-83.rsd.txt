Summary

The coronavirus 2019-nCoV is a biological weapon developed by the British, even if they pursued scientific goals.
On June 23, 2015, the U.S. Department of Justice granted scientists at the Pirbright Institute (formerly Animal Health Institute, England) the patent No.
10.130.701 for the invention of a “live attenuated coronavirus”.
So, molecular biologists saw structural details similar to the 2019-nCoV on the electron microscope image of the “British invention”.
